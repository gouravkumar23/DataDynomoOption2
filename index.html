FAQ

Good to know before you apply.

Who can participate?
+
Datanyx 2.0 is open to undergraduate & postgraduate students from any college across India. Basic familiarity with Python, ML, or analytics is recommended but not mandatory.
Is it a team event?
+
Yes. You can participate in teams of 2 to 4 members. Solo registrations are not accepted, but you can use our community channels to find teammates.
Do we need to bring our own laptops?
+
Yes, participants must carry their own laptops, chargers, and essential accessories. Wi-Fi and basic power extensions will be arranged at the venue.
Will food & stay be provided?
+
Overnight access to the venue with basic sleeping arrangements will be provided. Snacks, dinner, and breakfast will be arranged for all shortlisted teams.
What is the selection criteria?
+
Teams will be shortlisted based on problem-solving approach, relevance of skills, and clarity of ideas shared in the registration form. You‚Äôll receive an email if selected.
